About Me

I was rescued back in August 2014 when my previous owner couldn’t take care of me.

Here’s how that story went:

My mom originally wanted me but the owner said that I was given away already. In defeat, my mom settled for my fluffy nincompoop brother/cousin, Batman, instead. Long story short, the owner came clean and realized she didn’t have the capacity to take care of me since she already had a zoo. My mom was ecstatic and took me home to my now forever family.

I have a big brother, Tigger, who doesn’t like me very much but he tolerates me. There’s Onyx who is an old lady who doesn’t like to play with me, along with two dodo head dogs who can’t get enough of the treats I leave behind in my litter box. I love my family and if it wasn’t for them, I don’t know what life would be like.
